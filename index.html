01 / About

Field notes

I'm a product engineer with eight years split evenly between design and engineering teams, which mostly means I've learned to translate between the two. I care about interfaces that feel obvious in hindsight, and the architecture underneath that makes them fast and boring to maintain.

Before Northline, I led the design-systems team at a fintech startup through two rebrands and a Series C, and shipped accessibility improvements that took our product from failing audits to a reference case in our industry.

Off the clock: topographic maps, filter coffee, and a very slow chess habit.
